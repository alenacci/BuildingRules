We are conducting an academic experiment to gauge the ease of use of a new technology to manage Smart Buildings. The technology is a web application that allows you to specify policies you would want in your office. You can set policies like turn on office lights when it is raining, or turn on cooling when temperature is 75F. The software makes sure that you don't conflict other employees in the office or the policies set by the building manager. A more detailed explanation is given by a small video when you start the survey.

Select the link below to participate in the experiment.  This is a 7 day long experiment, where you will be participating for about 10 minutes each day.
